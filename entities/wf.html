Whisky Fish

This fish is distinguished by it's unusual rigidity and silvery metallic sheen. This fish is often full of whisky which can be consumed by anyone friendly with it.
The true origin of the fish is unknown, with the first citing occuring at Slave Bay near Meritt, British Columbia, Canada. This fish has a wide range, with the same individual spotted widely across the southern part of the province.
Although usually considered to contain whisky, this fish may also contain other spirits such as vodka.
Long term use of the Whisky Fish can lead to a drinking problem, as suffered by Ted Striker in the popular film AIRPLANE!
