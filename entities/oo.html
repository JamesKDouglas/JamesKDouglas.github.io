Oscillating Ocelots

This medium sized cat is so-named because the first discovered individual became known as "Ollie The Oscillating Oscillot Who Oscillates An Awful Lot". When excited or happy these cats visibly vibrate. Lower frequency vibration has been observed during resting conditions.
In personality the Oscillating Ocelots resemble Winnie The Pooh's friend Tigger. Although unable to actually bounce on their tail, they are known to use the phrase "Gotta Bounce!"
