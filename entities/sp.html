Sneezy Panda

Belonging to the true Pandas, this individual has an allergy to all grasses, including bamboo. Bamboo is their only food. Their sneezes can be heard for great distances, especially during preparation of their usual bamboo meals.
A discussion as to the impacts and ethics of offering antihistamines is ongoing. If offered, they will gratefully accept chlorpheniramine but, like humans, this compound can also make them very sleepy. Because of this, Sneezy Panda may be confused with the more common Sleepy Pandas.
